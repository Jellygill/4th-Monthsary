Make it look like an award-winning interactive art piece rather than a romantic greeting card.

Create a cinematic single-page interactive website called "The Heart That Keeps Beating."

This is NOT a Valentine's Day card, wedding invitation, scrapbook, or cute pink aesthetic website.

The goal is to create a deeply emotional and visually stunning experience about reassurance, resilience, staying together through difficult times, and unconditional love.

DESIGN DIRECTION

The website should feel like:

* A short animated film
* Elegant and modern
* Quiet and emotional
* Minimalist
* Premium quality
* Cinematic lighting
* Atmospheric and immersive

Avoid:

* Bright pink backgrounds
* Greeting card aesthetics
* Clipart hearts
* Bubble effects
* Decorative cursive fonts
* Excessive colors
* Anything that feels childish or templated

VISUAL STYLE

Background:

* Deep black fading into dark navy (#070B14)
* Subtle animated particles floating in the distance
* Very faint star-like specks
* Soft ambient glow

Heart Animation:

* The heart is the centerpiece.
* It is formed entirely from thousands of tiny glowing particles.
* The particles should look like fireflies or stardust.
* No visible heart outline.
* No bubbles.
* No cartoon effects.

The heart slowly forms from scattered particles.

Once formed:

* It gently beats like a real heartbeat.
* Tiny particles occasionally drift away.
* After a few moments they return.
* The heart never fully breaks.
* It continuously rebuilds itself.

This symbolizes enduring love despite hardships.

ATMOSPHERE

Everything should move slowly.

The experience should feel:

* peaceful
* comforting
* hopeful
* intimate
* mature

Think:
Interstellar.
A quiet night sky.
Fireflies gathering.
A heartbeat in the dark.

TEXT EXPERIENCE

Display only one message at a time.

The heart remains visible throughout.

Message 1:
"Some days are harder than others."

Fade out.

Message 2:
"Some days feel overwhelming."

Fade out.

Message 3:
"Some days things don't go the way we hoped."

Fade out.

Message 4:
"But even then..."

Pause.

Message 5:
"I'm still here."

The heart glows slightly brighter.

Pause.

FINAL SCENE

The heart becomes more stable.

Display:

Happy Monthsary, Honey ❤️

Below it:

"There may be a lot of things changing around us right now,
but my choice remains the same.

It will always be you, hon."

Typography:

* Elegant modern serif for headings
* Clean minimalist sans-serif for body text
* White text
* Soft glow
* Perfect spacing

FINAL EFFECT

The website does not end.

The heart continues beating forever.

Particles gently orbit around it.

Occasional sparkles drift upward.

The final message remains on screen.

The user should feel comforted, reassured, and loved.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

* Single HTML file
* HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only
* Canvas-based particle system
* Smooth animations
* Mobile responsive
* Modern glassmorphism effects only when subtle
* Professional-level visual quality
* Prioritize beauty and atmosphere over flashy effects
