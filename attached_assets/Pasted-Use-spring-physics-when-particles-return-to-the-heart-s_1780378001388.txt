PARTICLE SYSTEM AND ANIMATIONS

The particle system should be the visual centerpiece of the website.

Create thousands of tiny glowing particles using HTML5 Canvas.

Particle Appearance:

* Soft rose pink glow
* Slight variation in size
* Some particles brighter than others
* Firefly or stardust aesthetic
* No bubbles
* No circles with thick outlines
* No cartoon effects

HEART FORMATION

At the start:

* Particles are scattered randomly across the screen.
* They drift slowly and naturally.
* Over 5-8 seconds they begin converging.
* They gradually organize into a large heart shape at the center of the screen.
* The formation should feel magical and organic rather than mechanical.

The user should feel like the heart is being discovered rather than drawn.

HEART BEHAVIOR

Once formed:

* The heart gently beats every few seconds.
* Each heartbeat slightly expands and contracts the particle positions.
* The effect should be subtle and elegant.
* The heart should appear alive.

The heart must never feel completely static.

A small percentage of particles should occasionally drift away from the heart.

After a short time:

* Those particles naturally return.
* The heart reconstructs itself.
* The motion should be slow and graceful.

INTERACTION

Mouse movement and touch input should affect nearby particles.

When the cursor passes through the heart:

* Nearby particles are softly pushed away.
* The heart temporarily loses its shape.
* Particles scatter realistically.

After the cursor moves away:

* Particles slowly return to their assigned positions.
* The heart reforms naturally.
* The reconstruction should be smooth and satisfying.

The interaction should feel emotional and symbolic, not playful.

The user should feel like the heart is resilient rather than fragile.

MOTION QUALITY

Use easing functions and spring-like motion.

Avoid:

* Sharp movements
* Instant snapping
* Jittering
* Mechanical movement

Particles should glide smoothly and feel weightless.

ATMOSPHERIC EFFECTS

Around the heart:

* Small drifting particles float independently.
* Occasional sparkles appear and fade.
* A soft glow surrounds the heart.
* The glow subtly intensifies during each heartbeat.

The overall feeling should resemble:

* Fireflies gathering in the dark
* Stardust forming a constellation
* A living heart made of light
